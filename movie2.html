Contractor, The

Action,16V

James Harper, is involuntarily discharged from the U.S. Special Forces. To support his family, he joins a private contracting organisation alongside his best friend, under the command of a fellow veteran. Overseas on a covert mission, Harper must evade those trying to kill him while he makes his way back home.
